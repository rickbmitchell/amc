About

Andrew McQuiston is a graphic designer living and working in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Andrew McQuiston is a designer working in identity, print, and web design. In May 2011 he recieved a BFA in Graphic Design from the University of the Arts with a concentration in Book Arts & Printmaking.Currently based in Philadelphia, Andrew works as a UI designer for Urban Outfitters.
